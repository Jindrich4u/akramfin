Tactical IP Networks

Resilient mobile ad-hoc networking for contested environments

Self-Healing Network Architecture

Tactical IP networks employ mesh topology and intelligent routing protocols to maintain connectivity despite node failures, jamming, and geographic dispersion. Automatic route discovery and optimization ensure data reaches its destination through available paths.

Quality of service mechanisms prioritize mission-critical traffic while maximizing overall network throughput. Dynamic bandwidth allocation adapts to varying tactical situations and communication needs.

Network Features

  • Self-forming, self-healing mesh
  • Multi-hop routing capability
  • QoS traffic prioritization
  • Mobile node support up to 100 km/h
  • Scalable to thousands of nodes
  • IPv6 and legacy IPv4 support

Technical Specifications

  • Data rates: Up to 20 Mbps
  • Range: 5-50 km per hop
  • Latency: \u003c100 ms typical
  • Network reformation: \u003c2 seconds
  • Encryption: AES-256, FIPS 140-2
  • Frequency bands: VHF, UHF, L-band

Network Resilience

Jamming Resistance

Frequency hopping and adaptive modulation maintain connectivity under electronic attack. Automatic interference avoidance identifies clear spectrum and adjusts operating parameters.

Graceful Degradation

Network continues functioning despite node losses. Routing protocols automatically find alternate paths. Critical services maintained even with reduced capacity.

Rapid Recovery

Network topology adapts dynamically to changing conditions. New nodes integrate automatically. Damaged or jammed segments self-heal as conditions improve.
