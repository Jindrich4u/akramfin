### Quick Deploy (5 minutes)

1. **Go to Cloudflare Pages:**
   - Visit https://dash.cloudflare.com
   - Navigate to Workers & Pages → Pages

2. **Upload your site:**
   - Click "Upload assets"
   - Drag the entire `Qoderweb` folder
   - Click "Deploy"

3. **Done!**
   - Site live at: `https://your-project.pages.dev`
