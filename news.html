January 28, 2026

Auronex Medical Systems Devices Receive FDA Breakthrough Device Designation

The U.S. Food and Drug Administration has granted Breakthrough Device designation to Auronex's next-generation wearable cardiac monitoring system. The designation is reserved for medical devices that provide more effective treatment or diagnosis of life-threatening or irreversibly debilitating diseases.

The Breakthrough Device Program provides priority review and closer collaboration with FDA during the development and review process, potentially accelerating market entry by 12-18 months.
