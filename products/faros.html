Faros Cardiac Monitoring Device

Next-generation wearable ECG recorder for ambulatory cardiac diagnostics

Revolutionary Cardiac Monitoring

The Faros device represents the latest advancement in ambulatory ECG monitoring technology. Weighing just 15 grams, this ultra-compact recorder delivers hospital-grade signal quality in a form factor that disappears under clothing.

Wireless Bluetooth connectivity enables real-time data viewing and eliminates manual data download procedures. Rechargeable battery technology supports extended monitoring protocols up to 14 days on a single charge.

Key Features

  • Weight: 15 grams (including battery)
  • Up to 12-lead ECG recording
  • 1000 Hz sampling rate
  • 14-day battery life
  • IP67 water resistance
  • Bluetooth Low Energy wireless
  • Event marker button
  • 2 GB internal memory
