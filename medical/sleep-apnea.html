Home Sleep Apnea Testing

Convenient diagnostic solutions for sleep-disordered breathing evaluation

Accessible Sleep Diagnostics

Home sleep apnea testing (HSAT) provides accurate diagnosis of obstructive sleep apnea in the patient's natural sleep environment. Our portable systems deliver clinical-grade data while eliminating the need for costly laboratory-based polysomnography in appropriate patients.

Multi-channel recording captures respiratory effort, airflow, oxygen saturation, heart rate, and body position. Automated scoring algorithms provide rapid turnaround for diagnosis and treatment initiation.

System Capabilities

  • Type III sleep apnea testing
  • Multi-night recording capability
  • Integrated pulse oximetry
  • Nasal pressure and thermal airflow
  • Respiratory effort belts
  • Automatic event detection

Validated against laboratory polysomnography in clinical studies. High sensitivity and specificity for moderate to severe obstructive sleep apnea diagnosis.

Complete Testing Workflow

1. Patient Setup

Simple sensor application with illustrated instructions. Training video and phone support ensure proper setup.

2. Home Recording

Patient sleeps normally at home with device recording automatically. Multiple-night averaging improves diagnostic accuracy.

3. Data Analysis

Automated scoring with manual technician review. Comprehensive reports generated within 24-48 hours.

4. Diagnosis

Physician interpretation and treatment recommendation. Direct integration with therapy ordering systems.
