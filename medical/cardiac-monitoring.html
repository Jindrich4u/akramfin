Long-Term Cardiac Monitoring

Extended cardiac surveillance with real-time transmission capabilities

Extended Rhythm Analysis

Long-term cardiac monitoring extends observation periods beyond traditional Holter studies, increasing diagnostic yield for intermittent arrhythmias. Our systems support monitoring durations from weeks to months while maintaining patient comfort and compliance.

Wireless connectivity enables real-time data transmission to monitoring centers, allowing rapid clinical response to detected abnormalities. This capability is particularly valuable for high-risk patients requiring continuous surveillance.

System Features

  • 30-day to 90-day monitoring capability
  • Real-time rhythm transmission
  • Automated arrhythmia detection alerts
  • Patient-activated event recording
  • Secure cloud data storage
  • Mobile app for patient engagement

Clinical Indications

  • Atrial fibrillation screening
  • Cryptogenic stroke evaluation
  • Unexplained syncope investigation
  • Post-cardiac surgery monitoring
  • Antiarrhythmic drug efficacy
  • Implantable device pre-screening

Monitoring Center Integration

Seamless integration with third-party monitoring services or in-house monitoring programs. Customizable alert thresholds and notification protocols ensure appropriate clinical response.

Enhanced Patient Experience

Comfort

Ultra-lightweight patch design eliminates wires and allows normal showering and bathing. Adhesive technology ensures secure attachment throughout extended monitoring periods.

Engagement

Mobile application provides recording status, symptom logging, and educational content. Patient portal access enables viewing of monitoring progress.

Compliance

Simplified patient experience improves wearing compliance. Automated reminders and troubleshooting guidance reduce technical issues.
