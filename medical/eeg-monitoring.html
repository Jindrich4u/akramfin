Critical Care Neurophysiology

Continuous EEG monitoring in intensive care and emergency settings enables detection of non-convulsive seizures, assessment of encephalopathy, and prognostication in comatose patients. Our systems are optimized for rapid deployment and artifact-resistant recording in challenging clinical environments.

Wireless connectivity and battery operation allow bedside mobility without compromising signal quality. Real-time waveform visualization supports immediate clinical decision-making.

Clinical Applications

Optimized for Critical Care

Rapid Deployment

Pre-configured electrode sets and simplified application procedures enable non-specialist staff to initiate monitoring quickly. Color-coded connections prevent setup errors.

Artifact Resistance

Advanced filtering algorithms suppress electrical interference from ICU equipment. High common-mode rejection maintains signal integrity in electrically noisy environments.

Mobile Workflow

Wireless operation allows patient transport for imaging or procedures without interrupting monitoring. Automatic data synchronization maintains continuous recording.
