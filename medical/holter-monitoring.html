Holter Monitoring Solutions

Continuous ambulatory ECG monitoring for detecting cardiac arrhythmias

Comprehensive Cardiac Surveillance

Our Holter monitoring systems provide continuous ECG recording for extended periods, enabling detection of intermittent arrhythmias that may be missed during brief clinical examinations. Advanced signal processing ensures high-quality recordings even during normal patient activities.

Key Capabilities

  • Up to 14-day continuous recording
  • Hospital-grade signal quality
  • Water-resistant wearable design
  • Event marker for symptom correlation
  • Wireless data download
  • Rechargeable battery system

Clinical Applications

  • Arrhythmia detection and quantification
  • Syncope evaluation
  • Palpitation investigation
  • Post-ablation monitoring
  • Pacemaker function assessment
  • Heart rate variability analysis

Regulatory Clearances

  • FDA 510(k) cleared
  • CE Mark certified
  • ISO 13485 compliant manufacturing

Technical Specifications

Recording Performance

  • Sampling rate: 1000 Hz
  • Resolution: 16-bit ADC
  • Frequency response: 0.05-150 Hz
  • Input range: ±5 mV
  • Channels: Up to 12-lead

Device Specifications

  • Weight: 15 grams
  • Dimensions: 45 x 35 x 8 mm
  • Battery life: 14 days continuous
  • Storage: 2 GB internal memory
  • Water resistance: IP67 rated

Connectivity

  • Bluetooth Low Energy
  • USB data transfer
  • Wireless charging compatible
  • Cloud upload capability

Workflow Integration

Our Holter monitoring solutions integrate seamlessly into clinical workflows, from patient setup through final diagnostic reporting.

Setup

Simple electrode application and device attachment. Patient instructions provided for recording period.

Recording

Patient wears device during normal activities. Event marker records symptom occurrences.

Analysis

Automated arrhythmia detection and reporting. Manual review and editing capabilities.

Reporting

Comprehensive diagnostic reports with rhythm strips and statistical summaries.
